Mimir - Types of Threats

1. Ransomware

Imagine your computer files are locked and held hostage by a digital bandit. This cybercriminal demands money from you to release your files. It's like your computer is kidnapped, and you have to pay a ransom to set it free.

2. Phishing

Picture this: You receive an email that looks legit, like it's from your bank or a trusted website. But, surprise! It's actually from a sneaky imposter trying to trick you into sharing sensitive information, like passwords or credit card details. It's like fishing for your personal info with a fake bait.

3. Malware

Think of malware as the bad apples in the digital world. Malicious software (malware) can infect your computer and cause harm, like viruses or spyware. It's like a virtual disease that you want to avoid catching on your devices.

4. Social Engineering

Envision someone using psychological tricks rather than technical skills to manipulate you. Social engineers might pretend to be a friend, colleague, or even tech support to get you to reveal confidential information. It's like someone playing mind games to gain access to your personal data.
